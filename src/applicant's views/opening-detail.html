Dynamic Solution Innovators (DSi), started as a software company based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We have been successfully providing software services since 2001 in global industry (USA, England, New Zealand, Asia and Africa). We have 230+ employees, with over 200+ skilled engineers.

In 2020, DSi started a new business to serve the VLSI design and verification market and since then, we have received tremendous interest from clients and expanded our workforce gradually. We focus on the front-end portion of the VLSI design process, using SystemVerilog, UVM, System C, and numerous other cutting-edge technologies. We also work on the firmware and software stack required for a new microcontroller. We have trained and prepared engineers from scratch. Currently, we are seeking to expand our team and looking for Senior Design Verification Engineer who shall be responsible for the following-

Job Description
  • Complete verification ownership – Testbench Architecture, Test Plan and Testbench Development, Functional Coverage Closure, and Code Coverage Closure
  • Usage of industry-standard methodologies like UVM and constrained random approach to achieve verification goals
  • Actively explore and deploy techniques like Formal verification to achieve faster turnaround time on verification
  • Debug efficiently and clearly articulate gating issues to engineering managers.
  • Perform a technical lead role.
Requirements
  • Experience in architecting and implementing functional verification environments for complex IP and top-level.
  • Must have knowledge of these protocols: AXI, APB, AHB, Ethernet.
  • Good to have knowledge of one or more of these protocols: DDR/PCIE/ACE/CHI/SD/eMMC/USB.
  • Hands-on experience in creating Test Environments from Functional Specifications using UVM, Test Planning, Coverage closure, and Assertion based verifications.
  • Familiar with the tools and processes for developing testbenches and finishing all aspects of the verification process
  • Good scripting skills (Python, Perl, TCL) – being able to develop scripting to support new flows.
  • Able to provide verification solutions for productivity, performance, and throughput improvement.
  • Ability to achieve results in a fast-moving, agile flow and dynamic environment, both locally and across the organization.
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work well as a teammate.
  • Experience working with remote and international teams.
  • Ability to document and train others on work developed
Benefits
  • Work with some of the best and most experienced talents in the global VLSI Industry.
  • Opportunity to contribute in the revolution of Microprocessor design & development.
  • Training from foreign companies.
  • In-campus free lunch, snacks
  • Two festival bonuses
  • Yearly increments
